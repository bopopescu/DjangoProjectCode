Project Overview

My project is a web application that gathers continues unprompted feedback from uploaded online presentations. The application will analyse and correlate audience feedback information to identify level of audience engagement and understanding of the presented material using statistical methods.

Research question

Prove the hypothesis that when students give continues unprompted feedback on a presentation/lecture this Improves the Teaching of the material. Thus Improve the overall learning and understanding of the material.
